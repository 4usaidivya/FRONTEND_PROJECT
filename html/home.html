Agriculture Farming

“Agriculture”is the science and art of cultivating plants and livestock. Agriculture was the key development in the rise of sedentary human civilization.

Harvest Farming

“Harvesting” is the process of gathering a ripe crop from the fields. Reaping is the cutting of grain or pulse for harvest, typically using a scythe, sickle, or reaper.

Dairy Farming

“Dairy farming” is a class of agriculture for long-term production of milk, which is processed (either on the farm or at a dairy plant, either of which may be called a dairy) for eventual sale of a dairy product.

Saline Agriculture

“Sea salt ”is salt that is produced by the evaporation of seawater. It is used as a seasoning in foods, cooking, cosmetics and for preserving food.
